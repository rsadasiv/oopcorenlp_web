OOPCorpusDocumentZScoresViewer

Title: OOP Corpus Document ZScores Viewer
URI: OOPCorpusDocumentZScoresViewerr
Routing: servlets/OOPCorpusDocumentZScoresViewer.java -> jsp/OOPCorpusDocumentZScoresViewer.jsp -> js/OOPCorpusDocumentZScoresViewer.js
Parameters: Corpus, Document, TargetCorpus, AggregateName
Content type: text/html
Links: OOPCorpusDocumentAnnotationZScoresViewer

Displays the z-score for each annotation in the document against the selected target corpus in a vega-lite bar chart. Normalized is the most interpretable aggregate name.
Lists the Cosine Similarity score between the document and all corpora. Mouse-over anywhere in the chart activates the vega-embed action menu (circle with three dots on far right). Mouse-over a bar shows the data point value. Clicking on a bar navigates to OOPCorpusDocumentAnnotationZScoresViewer.
