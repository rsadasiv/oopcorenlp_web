CorpusDocumentsViewer

Title: Corpus Documents Viewer
URI: CorpusDocumentsViewer
Routing: servlets/CorpusDocumentsViewer.java -> jsp/CorpusDocumentsViewer.jsp
Parameters: Corpus
Content type: text/html
Links: TextViewer, rest/browse/Corpora/corpus/document/PIPELINE

Displays the list of available documents with thumbnail image and title as well as the and the top level annotation Cosine Similarity score between the document and the corpus. Document title links to pipeline json; similarity score links to TextViewer.
