OOPDocumentViewer

OOPCoreNLP
URI: OOPDocumentViewer
Routing: servlets/OOPDocumentViewer.java -> OOPDocumentViewer.jsp
Parameters: Corpus, Document
Content type: text/html
Output: All annotation and subannotations displayed as a color coded overlay on Medium-like CSS. Annotations and subannotations are displayed on the left in collapsable menus. Click on a token to see the raw json of all token scores. Click on a period to see the raw json of all sentence scores. To toggle the overlay click the selected color in the color palette next to the annotation or click on the color text at the top of the page.
