OOPLexiconViewer

Title: OOP Lexicon Viewer
URI: OOPLexiconViewer
Routing: servlets/OOPLexiconViewer.java -> jsp/OOPLexiconViewer.jsp -> js/OOPLexiconViewer.js
Parameters: Corpus, Document, Subannotation
Content type: text/html

Displays all annotations for a selected word (subannotation of OOPWordsAnnotation) in the document lexicon.
Displays topic models for the selected work in the document.
The vega-lite chart shows occurrences of the selected word by sentence.
Mouse-over anywhere in the chart activates the vega-embed action menu (circle with three dots on far right).
Mouse-over a bar shows the data point value.
Click a bar to see the sentence in context.
