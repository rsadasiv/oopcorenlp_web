CorpusCVScoresViewer

Title: OOP Corpus CV Scores Viewer
URI: OOPCorpusCVScoresViewer
Routing: servlets/OOPCorpusCVScoresViewer.java -> jsp/OOPCorpusCVScoresViewer.jsp -> js/OOPCorpusCVScoresViewer.js
Parameters: Corpus
Content type: text/html
Links: CorpusAnnotationCVScoresViewer

Displays the Coefficient of Variation for each annotation across the corpus in a vega-lite bar chart. The lower the better. Mouse-over anywhere in the chart activates the vega-embed action menu (circle with three dots on far right). Mouse-over a bar shows the data point value. Clicking on a bar navigates to CorpusAnnotationCVScoresViewer.
